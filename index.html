About US

The Chandigarh University Astronomy Club is a vibrant and dynamic student oriented club dedicated to fostering a passion for astronomy and space exploration. Established within the Aerospace Engineering Department, the club serves as a platform for students to engage in various astronomical activities, gain knowledge, and explore the wonders of the universe.

ABOUT GRAHAGAAMI

Grahgaami (ग्रहगामी) is a Hindi term that is translated to a Celestial Traveler or Cosmic Traveler in English. It denotes an entity that traverses through the celestial realm, such as a celestial object, spacecraft, or even an idea exploring the cosmos. The domains in our field represent the various concepts which contribute to the Cosmos. It represents Innovation, Discovery and Exploration.
