CHANDIGARH UNIVERSITY:

Chandigarh University (CU) is a leading Indian Institution offering its students a unique amalgamation of professional and academic excellence. The University has been accredited with the prestigious A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Chandigarh University has become Youngest and the only private university in India to bag an A+ grade in the first cycle of the accreditation process and has also become the only state private university of Punjab to be accredited by NAAC.

Introduction:

The Chandigarh University Astronomy Club is a vibrant and dynamic student oriented club dedicated to fostering a passion for astronomy and space exploration. Established within the Aerospace Engineering Department, the club serves as a platform for students to engage in various astronomical activities, gain knowledge, and explore the wonders of the universe.

know more

Club's Mission:

The club's primary mission is to promote the study and understanding of astronomy among students at Chandigarh University. It aims to create an inclusive and supportive community that encourages curiosity, scientific inquiry, and appreciation for the cosmos. By organizing events, workshops, and stargazing sessions, the club strives to inspire and educate students about the vastness of space and the mysteries it holds.
